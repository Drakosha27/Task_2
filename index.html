About ILLIT

ILLIT is a South Korean girl group under BELIFT LAB (HYBE). The five members — Yunah, Minju, Moka, Wonhee, and Iroha — debuted in March 2024 with the EP SUPER REAL ME. The title track “Magnetic” went viral worldwide and brought the group major rookie recognition.

Achievements

  • Final lineup formed via the survival show “R U Next?” (2023)
  • Debut EP SUPER REAL ME released in March 2024
  • “Magnetic” viral dance trend and strong global charting
  • Music show win and multiple rookie award nominations in 2024
